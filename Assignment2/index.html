About

Paige Falter was born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio. She is currently 19 years old as of this past September. She went to Indian Hill Highschool for all thirteen years of her schooling experience, activley participating in multiple sports including soccer, lacrosse, and hockey. She also has a ranging history in theatre, participating in the theatre program for seven years in highschool. She wrote and directed her own show "Maize" senior year before she graduated. "Maize" was originally a video game idea about a young girl attmepting to get out of a coma, it was through this show that Paige realized what she wanted to do with her future. She now goes to Miami University where she is majoring in Games and Simulations in hopes of becoming a 3D artist, story writer, and character designer for the bigshot game making studios one day. Outside of schooling, Paige enjoys playing hockey, going out with her friends, reading, being active, drawing, painting, and writing! You might often find her at the makerspace where she user the laser cutter to make her own earrings.
